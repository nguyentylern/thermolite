Thermolite

Secure food carriage. An EatEZ Design.

Designed to make delivery easy.

Compact yet still size efficient, Thermolite provides the ease of use to its users and the recipients of its contents.

Thermolite is a food delivery container designed to help regulate the temperature of meals and to help its users to identify who the meals might be for and/or what might be inside. It was created for the organization, EatEZ, to assist their volunteers in delivering prepared meals to seniors.
